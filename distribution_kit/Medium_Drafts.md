Instead of copy-pasting, use Medium's **"Import a story"** feature ([medium.com/p/import](https://medium.com/p/import)).
1. Paste the URL of your live article (e.g., `https://bharath-saga.guvvala.xyz/articles/speed-of-light-revati-puranas`).
2. Medium will automatically pull the content and, most importantly, set a **Canonical Link**.
3. This tells Google your website is the original source, protecting your SEO!
